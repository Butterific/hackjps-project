How To Play?



First of all, thanks for visiting our game.

Now the main question arises whenever you play a new game and that is: How Do You Play This Game?

Well, this is what this page is about.

Let's Start

Our game starts off with a home page where it says "Play" and "How to Play". When you click on 'Play', it takes you to a new page, where you actually play the game. You can pick your difficulty, whether it's easy, medium, or hard. Based on the difficulty you picked, the AI generates a scenario for you. You read the scenario and then type in your strategy. After you type in your strategy, you submit it. Based on that, the AI will tell you if you'll survive in this scenario or die. The moment you click 'NEW GAME', your timer starts. Based on the difficulty you picked, the time decreases. For example, for 'hard' difficulty, you have 60 seconds to type in your strategy and then hit submit. If your time is up and you don't hit submit, you die automatically. Then you click 'New Game' if you want to play again, and the whole process happens all over again, but with another scenario. All of these scenarios are randomly picked and generated through AI.

The game is quite easy and self-explanatory.
